# Chicken Tikka Masala

## Ingredients
* 1 kg chicken breast, cut into chunks
* 1 cup plain yogurt
* 2 tbsp lemon juice
* 2 tsp garam masala
* 1 tsp ground cumin
* 1 tsp ground coriander
* 1 tsp cayenne pepper
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 inch ginger, grated
* 2 tbsp vegetable oil
* 1 large onion, diced
* 400g canned tomatoes, crushed
* 1 cup heavy cream
* 2 tbsp tomato paste
* 1 tsp paprika
* Salt to taste
* Fresh cilantro for garnish

## Procedure
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, 1 tsp garam masala, cumin, coriander, cayenne, half the garlic, and ginger for at least 30 minutes.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Cook marinated chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes.
4. Remove chicken and set aside.
5. In the same skillet, saut√© onion until softened, about 5 minutes.
6. Add remaining garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute.
7. Stir in tomato paste, remaining garam masala, and paprika.
8. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
9. Stir in cream and return chicken to the skillet.
10. Simmer for 5 minutes until sauce thickens.
11. Season with salt and garnish with fresh cilantro.
12. Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.
