# Cornstarch Alfajores - Alfajores de maicena

## Ingredients
* (Makes 20–24 small alfajores)
* 200 g unsalted butter, at room temperature
* 150 g sugar
* 3 egg yolks
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* Zest of 1 lemon (optional but highly recommended)
* 200 g all-purpose flour
* 300 g cornstarch
* 2 teaspoons baking powder
* A pinch of salt
* 400 g dulce de leche repostero (thick dulce de leche suitable for baking/filling)
* Shredded coconut, as needed

## Procedure

1. Cream butter and sugar (beat until light and fluffy).
2. Add the egg yolks, vanilla, and lemon zest. Mix well.
3. Add dry ingredients Sift together the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and salt.
4. Add to the butter mixture and combine gently. Do not knead; just bring the dough together until uniform.
5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
6. Roll out the dough to about half centimeter thickness.
7. Cut out rounds 4–5 cm in diameter.
8. Place on an ungreased baking sheet.
9. Bake in a low oven, 160–170°C, for 10–15 minutes: They should remain pale; do not let them brown.
10. Assemble time! Let cookies cool completely. It is better to let them cool in the fridge for 24 hours.
11. Sandwich two cookies with a generous layer of dulce de leche.
12. Roll the edges in shredded coconut.
