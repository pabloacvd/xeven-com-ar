# Classic Beef Stew

## Ingredients
* 2 lbs beef chuck, cut into 2-inch cubes
* 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
* 2 tbsp vegetable oil
* 1 large onion, chopped
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 3 tbsp tomato paste
* 1/2 cup red wine
* 4 cups beef broth
* 2 bay leaves
* 1 tsp dried thyme
* 4 large carrots, cut into chunks
* 3 large potatoes, cubed
* 2 celery stalks, chopped
* 1 cup frozen peas
* Salt and pepper to taste
* 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

## Procedure
1. Season beef cubes with salt and pepper, then coat with flour.
2. Heat oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
3. Brown beef cubes on all sides, working in batches if necessary.
4. Remove beef and set aside.
5. In the same pot, saut√© onion until softened, about 5 minutes.
6. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook for 1 minute.
7. Pour in red wine to deglaze, scraping up any browned bits.
8. Return beef to pot and add broth, bay leaves, and thyme.
9. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer covered for 1.5 hours.
10. Add carrots, potatoes, and celery.
11. Continue simmering for 30-45 minutes until vegetables are tender.
12. Stir in frozen peas and cook for 5 minutes.
13. Remove bay leaves and adjust seasoning.
14. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
