# Authentic Spaghetti Carbonara

## Ingredients
* 400g spaghetti
* 200g guanciale or pancetta, diced
* 4 large egg yolks
* 1 whole egg
* 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
* 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
* Fresh black pepper
* Salt for pasta water

## Procedure
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package directions.
2. While pasta cooks, heat a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Add guanciale and cook until crispy and golden, about 5-7 minutes.
4. In a bowl, whisk together egg yolks, whole egg, and both cheeses.
5. Add plenty of freshly ground black pepper to the egg mixture.
6. When pasta is almost done, reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water.
7. Drain pasta and immediately add to the skillet with guanciale.
8. Remove from heat and quickly toss with the egg mixture.
9. Add pasta water gradually while tossing to create a creamy sauce.
10. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.
